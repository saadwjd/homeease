FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service? +

Download the HomeEase app, browse providers by category, select one that suits you, and book a time slot. You'll get a confirmation notification once the provider accepts.

Are providers verified? +

Yes. Every provider goes through CNIC verification and a background check before their profile goes live. Verified providers have a blue checkmark on their profile.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept JazzCash for online payments, or you can choose to pay cash directly to the provider after the service is completed.

How do I become a provider? +

Download the app, go to Settings → Become a Service Provider, fill in your details, and submit. Our team reviews applications within 48 hours.

Which cities do you serve? +

We currently operate in Lahore. We're expanding to Karachi, Islamabad, and other major cities very soon. Stay tuned!
